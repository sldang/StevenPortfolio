    Overview

    A custom apparel project featuring original typography and graphics created in Adobe Illustrator, then translated into physical garments using heat-transfer vinyl. The design process included developing vector artwork, refining layout and composition for the back graphic and pocket patch, and preparing files for cutting. Production involved operating a vinyl cutter, weeding the cut graphics, and using a heat press to accurately position and apply the designs to the garments for a clean, durable finish.
