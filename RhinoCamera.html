    Project Four

    A Creative Design Project

    Skills

    Low-Fidelity Prototyping, 3D Modeling, 2D Modeling

    Tools

    Rhino, Laser Cutting, 3D Printing, Low-Fidelity Prototyping

    Overview

    I first created a Low-Fidelity Prototype of the Fujifilm XT-4 out of common materials such as cardboard and styrofoam as a proof of concept. We then transitioned into creating a 2D Design of the object to be ready to laser cut. From this 2D Design, I would 3D model the camera to be 3D printed.
