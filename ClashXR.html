    Overview

    Clash XR is a fast-paced Mixed Reality (MR) game designed to gamify and improve hand-eye coordination. It was developed as a remake of Superhot VR for the Supercell AI Hackathon. Players use an X-Bow (Meta Quest Controllers) to shoot non-stop, Supercell-inspired enemy hordes, including the Witch and Skeletons, in a physically engaging environment. The project utilized advanced tools for rapid development, including Hexagen for 3D model generation, Blender for rigging, Meta SDK/Unity for compilation, and Gemini/ChatGPT for AI-assisted coding.
